About ShipDecKK

Learn about our company, mission, and the team behind our success

Our Story

Founded by a passionate group of maritime professionals and industry experts, ShipDecKK was created with a clear goal: to transform the ship sales experience by combining premium-quality ships with outstanding customer service.

What started as a modest venture has rapidly grown into a well-regarded entity within the maritime world, offering a wide range of vessels from luxurious yachts to commercial cargo ships.

Today, ShipDecKK continues to grow and diversify its offerings, maintaining a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, serving maritime clients worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide outstanding maritime vessels that surpass expectations in every aspect—quality, performance, and design—while offering exceptional customer service throughout the entire ownership experience.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in maritime solutions, recognized for excellence, innovation, and integrity in all aspects of our business.

Our Company Values

Excellence

Our Achievements

Since its founding in 2020, ShipDecKK has quickly become a respected name in the maritime industry, known for delivering exceptional vessels and unmatched customer service. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us prestigious awards and recognition from industry leaders, solidifying our reputation as a trusted provider of luxury yachts and commercial ships. We continue to grow and innovate, ensuring that our clients receive the best in both products and services.
